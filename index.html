How to read this:

This diagram visualizes our Totally Stubby configuration:

  • The ABR (Core-1) actively filters out all external and inter-area LSAs.
  • The Default Route (O*IA) is the only "outside" knowledge allowed into the Area.
  • This ensures our edge switches only carry a tiny, stable routing table.
